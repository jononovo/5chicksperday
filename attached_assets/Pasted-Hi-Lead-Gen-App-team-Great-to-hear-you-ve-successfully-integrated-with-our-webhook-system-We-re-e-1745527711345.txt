The most important fields are:

searchId: A unique identifier for this search (we use this to track progress)
status: "completed" for final webhooks (or "in_progress" for progress updates)
progress: 100 for final webhooks (or 10, 35, 70, etc. for progress updates)
results.companies: An array of company objects with the required fields
results.metadata: Additional information about the search and validation scores
For data storage, focus on the company objects within the results.companies array. Each object includes both required fields (name, website, industry) and optional fields (location, description, employeeCount, etc.) that provide additional context.
